About Me

I’m just an IT guy who started my journey with the QNX operating system—long before I ever used Windows. I learned a lot working with QNX, and for those who remember the Photon Desktop Manager, it was truly ahead of its time!

Eventually, Windows came along, but I quickly transitioned to FreeBSD. I ended up using both systems for quite a while. I spent many years as a sysadmin before moving into project management. Today, I’m a Project Director, which has taken me far from the technical side of things.

Still, my passion for tinkering with servers never went away. Recently, I’ve started learning new technologies again and decided to document my journey here.
